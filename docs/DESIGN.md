# Syntrix Design System

## Philosophy

Syntrix is not dashboard software.

Syntrix is an AI Operating System for business leaders.

Every screen should answer one question:

"What should I do next?"

---

# Design Principles

## 1. Clarity over Complexity

Executives should understand the screen within five seconds.

Never overload the user with unnecessary charts.

---

## 2. Intelligence over Information

Data alone has little value.

Every important metric should explain:

• Why it changed
• Why it matters
• What action should be taken

---

## 3. Opportunity over Problems

Never focus only on what's wrong.

Always surface the highest-value opportunity.

Instead of:

Worst Store

Use:

Biggest Opportunity

---

## 4. Confidence

Syntrix speaks like an experienced COO.

Not a chatbot.

Never:

"Hello! How may I assist you?"

Instead:

"Labor exceeded target by 4.1%.

Recommend reducing Friday dinner staffing by one server."

---

## 5. Calm

No flashing colors.

No unnecessary notifications.

Only show what matters.
