# The Syntrix Platform

Mission

Help businesses make better operational decisions.

---

The Platform consists of three engines.

## Intelligence Engine

Observe the business.

Generate signals.

Understand why things happen.

Predict what will happen.

---

## Decision Engine

Transform intelligence into decisions.

Brief leaders.

Prioritize opportunities.

Recommend actions.

Measure business impact.

---

## Automation Engine

Execute approved decisions.

Integrate with business systems.

Verify outcomes.

Learn from results.
