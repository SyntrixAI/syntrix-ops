# Syntrix Architecture

## Vision

Syntrix is an AI Executive Operating System for multi-location businesses.

Rather than presenting dashboards full of metrics, Syntrix continuously observes operations, explains what matters, recommends the next best action, and learns from outcomes.

The goal is simple:

> Help leaders make better operational decisions every day.

---

# Core Philosophy

Every page should help the user make exactly one better decision.

Everything else exists to support that decision.

# Business Engines

Business engines answer one specific business question.

Examples:

- Signal Engine
- Decision Engine
- Root Cause Engine
- Trend Engine
- Recommendation Engine
- Execution Engine
- Hierarchy Engine
- Metrics Engine

Engines never render UI.

They never know about pages.

They simply answer business questions.

---

# Intelligence Layer

The Intelligence layer combines multiple engine outputs into executive reasoning.

Examples:

- Executive Brief
- Recommendation Context
- Opportunity Ranking

Future:

- Daily Brief Builder
- Learning Summary
- Business Narrative

Intelligence synthesizes.

It does not own business data.

---

# Workspace Services

Workspace services prepare data for one workspace.

Examples:

- Daily Brief
- Organization
- Operations
- Execution
- Intelligence

Services assemble.

They do not perform business reasoning.

# Product Principle

Syntrix should never ask executives to interpret dozens of metrics.

Syntrix should interpret the business first.

The executive should begin with conclusions and drill into evidence only when needed.
